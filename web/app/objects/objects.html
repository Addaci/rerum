RERUM accepts new objects based on their @type property. Though we plan to resolve the @context of contributions for now these terms are required.

The following object types are currently allowed in RERUM:

Annotations:
oa:Annotation, http://www.w3.org/ns/oa#Annotation
Manifests:
iiif:Manifest, sc:Manifest, http://iiif.io/api/presentation/2#Manifest
Canvases:
iiif:Canvas, sc:Canvas, http://iiif.io/api/presentation/2#Canvas
List Objects:
sc:Sequence, sc:AnnotationList, sc:Range, ore:Aggregation
Text Resources:
cnt:chars
Agents:
foaf:Agent, foaf:Person, foaf:Group, foaf:Organization, foaf:Project

We are working to include others. Let us know if you have a special need.
